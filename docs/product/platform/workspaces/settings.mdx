Navigate to **Settings** in the sidebar to manage your workspace. Settings are grouped into tabs: General, Team, Root Keys, Security, and Billing.

### Workspace name

The display name for your workspace (3 to 50 characters). This is not customer-facing. It helps you and your team identify the workspace in the dashboard.

1. Navigate to **Settings > General**.
2. Enter a new name in the **Workspace Name** field.
3. Click **Save**.

### Workspace ID

A read-only, system-generated identifier for your workspace (for example, `ws_abc123`). Click the copy button to copy it to your clipboard. You need this ID when calling the Unkey API.

## Security

Use **Settings > Security** to manage sign-in security for your own dashboard account.

### Two-factor authentication

Two-factor authentication requires a 6 digit code from an authenticator app when you sign in. Use it when you want extra protection for the dashboard account that can access workspace settings, root keys, billing, or production deployments.

To enable two-factor authentication:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Security**.
2. Click **Add authenticator app**.
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app.
4. If you can't scan the QR code, copy the setup code and enter it manually.
5. Enter the 6 digit code from your authenticator app.
6. Click **Verify and enable**.

To remove an authenticator app, return to **Settings > Security** and click **Remove** next to the configured factor.
